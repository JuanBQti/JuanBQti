# Hi 👋

I’m a PhD economist specializing in mechanism design and game theory, focused on incentive systems in crypto—tokenomics, staking/validator economics, governance, and auctions.

I help teams design and stress-test mechanisms that translate cleanly into implementable protocol parameters: reward and fee schemes, delegation and voting incentives, and market design for scarce resources (e.g., blockspace) under strategic and adversarial behavior.

My work sits at the intersection of theory and production constraints: I build rigorous models, derive equilibrium predictions, and validate them with data and simulations to support decisions that survive real users and real incentives.

**What I do**
- Tokenomics & incentive design (emissions, rewards, fees, liquidity incentives)
- Governance & delegation mechanisms (participation, concentration, sybil resistance)
- Auction/market design (pricing & allocation of scarce resources, MEV-style competition)
- Quant + empirical validation (simulation, backtesting, data analysis)
